Meet LangQuest — Connect. Learn. Grow Together.

Problem: UTM English Club faces low engagement due to disorganized communication, manual member management, and unmotivating activity experiences.

Students often miss event updates as announcements get buried in WhatsApp and Telegram, leading to low participation. Organizers also rely on manual Excel sheets to track attendance, which is time-consuming, error-prone, and provides little insight into engagement. Existing tools lack structured event management and interactive learning, resulting in poor coordination and low long-term motivation.

◆ A first-of-its-kind game-based learning and event promotion platform for university students that makes studying engaging and interactive, while giving administrators a powerful dashboard to track attendance and user engagement in real time.
